Frequently Asked Questions

What permissions does Lightning BD need?

Lightning BD requests the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • Google Sheets™: To read your contact data and update email status
  • Gmail Send: To send personalized emails from your account
  • Google Drive (Read-only): To list and select your Google Sheets™ files

Is my data secure?

Yes. Lightning BD:

  • Only accesses the Google Sheets™ files you explicitly select
  • Never stores your contact data on our servers
  • Uses secure OAuth authentication
  • Follows Google's security best practices

How does email tracking work?

When you send emails through Lightning BD, the app automatically updates your Google Sheets™ with:

  • Email status (Sent, Failed)
  • Date sent
  • Template used
  • Gmail thread ID for reference

Can I customize email templates?

Absolutely! Edit the templates in the "Setup" sheet of your Lightning BD Google Sheets™ template. You can:

  • Modify existing templates
  • Add new templates
  • Use variables like {firstName}, {company}, etc. for personalization

What if I encounter an error?

If you experience any issues:

  • Check that your Google Sheets™ template has the correct column headers
  • Ensure your Gmail account is properly connected
  • Verify that email addresses are valid
  • Contact us at keys@keys-marketing.com with details about the error

Troubleshooting

App Won't Connect to Google

If you're having trouble connecting your Google account:

  • Make sure you're using the same Google account for both Sheets and Gmail
  • Clear your browser cache and try again
  • Disable any browser extensions that might block OAuth
  • Try using an incognito/private browser window

Emails Not Sending

If emails aren't being sent:

  • Check that email addresses are properly formatted
  • Verify your Gmail account has sending permissions
  • Ensure you haven't exceeded Gmail's daily sending limits
  • Check that required contact fields (email, firstName) are filled

Can't See My Google Sheets™

If your sheets aren't appearing in the app:

  • Make sure the sheet is accessible to your connected Google account
  • Check that the sheet has the correct Lightning BD template structure
  • Try refreshing the app or logging out and back in
