Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

Lightning BD ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Google Sheets add-on.

Information We Access

Lightning BD requires certain permissions to function properly. When you install and use our add-on, we access:

Google Sheets Data

  • Contact information you enter in your spreadsheet (names, email addresses, company names)
  • Email templates you create within the Setup tab
  • Email status tracking data that the add-on generates

Gmail Data

  • Ability to compose and send emails on your behalf
  • Access to your Gmail signature for email composition
  • Email thread IDs for follow-up functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the accessed information solely to provide the Lightning BD service:

  • To personalize and send emails based on your templates
  • To track email sending status within your spreadsheet
  • To manage email threads for follow-up sequences
  • To display your contacts in the email editor interface

Data Storage and Security

Important: Lightning BD does not store any of your data on external servers.

  • All data remains within your Google account (Sheets and Gmail)
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your contact lists or email content to any external servers
  • Email templates and contact data stay in your Google Sheets
  • The add-on operates entirely within Google's infrastructure

Analytics and Tracking

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve our service:

  • Number of times the add-on is opened
  • Number of emails sent (not the content)
  • Feature usage statistics (e.g., bulk draft creation)
  • Error logs for troubleshooting (no personal data included)

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Lightning BD operates within Google's ecosystem and relies on:

  • Google Sheets API: To read and write spreadsheet data
  • Gmail API: To send emails and manage threads
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Data Retention

Since Lightning BD doesn't store your data:

  • Your spreadsheet data remains under your control in Google Sheets
  • Emails sent are stored in your Gmail account
  • You can delete your data at any time by removing it from your spreadsheet
  • Uninstalling the add-on immediately stops all access to your data

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in your Google Sheets and Gmail
  • Modification: Edit or delete any data directly in your spreadsheet
  • Revocation: Remove the add-on's access anytime via Google account settings
  • Portability: Export your spreadsheet data using Google Sheets' built-in features

Children's Privacy

Lightning BD is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
